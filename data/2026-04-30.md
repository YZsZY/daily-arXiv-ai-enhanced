### [1] [Unified 4D World Action Modeling from Video Priors with Asynchronous Denoising](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.26694)
*Jun Guo,Qiwei Li,Peiyan Li,Zilong Chen,Nan Sun,Yifei Su,Heyun Wang,Yuan Zhang,Xinghang Li,Huaping Liu*

Main category: cs.RO

TL;DR: X-WAM是一个统一的4D世界模型，结合了实时机器人动作执行和高保真4D世界合成（视频+3D重建），通过异步噪声采样（ANS）和轻量级结构适配，在RoboCasa和RoboTwin 2.0基准上分别达到79.2%和90.7%的平均成功率。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 现有统一世界模型（如UWM）仅建模2D像素空间，无法在动作执行效率与世界建模质量之间取得平衡，限制了机器人对4D空间的感知与交互能力。

Method: 1) 利用预训练视频扩散模型预测多视角RGB-D视频；2) 轻量级结构适配：复制扩散Transformer最后几个块作为深度预测分支；3) 异步噪声采样（ANS）：推理时采用非同步去噪调度，快速解码动作用于实时执行，同时用完整步骤生成高保真视频。

Result: 在超过5800小时机器人数据上预训练，RoboCasa基准平均成功率79.2%，RoboTwin 2.0基准平均成功率90.7%，在视觉和几何指标上均优于现有方法。

Conclusion: X-WAM成功实现了4D世界建模与实时动作执行的统一，验证了异步噪声采样策略在平衡效率与质量方面的有效性。

Abstract: We propose X-WAM, a Unified 4D World Model that unifies real-time robotic action execution and high-fidelity 4D world synthesis (video + 3D reconstruction) in a single framework, addressing the critical limitations of prior unified world models (e.g., UWM) that only model 2D pixel-space and fail to balance action efficiency and world modeling quality. To leverage the strong visual priors of pretrained video diffusion models, X-WAM imagines the future world by predicting multi-view RGB-D videos, and obtains spatial information efficiently through a lightweight structural adaptation: replicating the final few blocks of the pretrained Diffusion Transformer into a dedicated depth prediction branch for the reconstruction of future spatial information. Moreover, we propose Asynchronous Noise Sampling (ANS) to jointly optimize generation quality and action decoding efficiency. ANS applies a specialized asynchronous denoising schedule during inference, which rapidly decodes actions with fewer steps to enable efficient real-time execution, while dedicating the full sequence of steps to generate high-fidelity video. Rather than entirely decoupling the timesteps during training, ANS samples from their joint distribution to align with the inference distribution. Pretrained on over 5,800 hours of robotic data, X-WAM achieves 79.2% and 90.7% average success rate on RoboCasa and RoboTwin 2.0 benchmarks, while producing high-fidelity 4D reconstruction and generation surpassing existing methods in both visual and geometric metrics.

### [2] [3D Generation for Embodied AI and Robotic Simulation: A Survey](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.26509)
*Tianwei Ye,Yifan Mao,Minwen Liao,Jian Liu,Chunchao Guo,Dazhao Du,Quanxin Shou,Fangqi Zhu,Song Guo*

Main category: cs.RO

TL;DR: 本篇综述首次系统梳理了面向具身AI的3D生成技术，围绕数据生成、仿真环境构建和Sim2Real迁移三大角色组织文献，指出该领域正从视觉真实感转向交互就绪性，并识别了物理标注不足、几何质量与物理有效性脱节、评估碎片化及仿真到真实鸿沟等关键瓶颈。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 具身AI和机器人系统需要可扩展、多样化、具备物理真实感的3D内容用于仿真训练和真实部署，而现有3D生成技术主要关注视觉真实感，缺乏对运动学结构、材质属性、场景交互能力等具身任务需求的满足，亟需系统性的综述来梳理现状、挑战和未来方向。

Method: 通过系统文献调研，将现有3D生成技术组织为三大角色：数据生成器（生成可仿真资产，包括铰接式、可变形等物理内容）、仿真环境（构建可交互、面向任务的世界，涵盖结构感知、可控及智能体场景生成）、Sim2Real桥梁（支持数字孪生重建、数据增强及合成演示用于机器人学习与真实迁移）。

Result: 发现该领域正从视觉真实感转向交互就绪性；识别出关键瓶颈包括：物理标注数据有限、几何质量与物理有效性之间存在鸿沟、评估标准碎片化、以及持续的仿真到真实域差距。

Conclusion: 3D生成要成为具身智能的可靠基础，必须解决物理标注不足、几何与物理脱节、评估不统一和Sim2Real差距等核心问题，从而推动从视觉真实感到交互就绪的范式转变。

Abstract: Embodied AI and robotic systems increasingly depend on scalable, diverse, and physically grounded 3D content for simulation-based training and real-world deployment. While 3D generative modeling has advanced rapidly, embodied applications impose requirements far beyond visual realism: generated objects must carry kinematic structure and material properties, scenes must support interaction and task execution, and the resulting content must bridge the gap between simulation and reality. This survey presents the first survey of 3D generation for embodied AI and organizes the literature around three roles that 3D generation plays in embodied systems. In \emph{Data Generator}, 3D generation produces simulation-ready objects and assets, including articulated, physically grounded, and deformable content for downstream interaction; in \emph{Simulation Environments}, it constructs interactive and task-oriented worlds, spanning structure-aware, controllable, and agentic scene generation; and in \emph{Sim2Real Bridge}, it supports digital twin reconstruction, data augmentation, and synthetic demonstrations for downstream robot learning and real-world transfer. We also show that the field is shifting from visual realism toward interaction readiness, and we identify the main bottlenecks, including limited physical annotations, the gap between geometric quality and physical validity, fragmented evaluation, and the persistent sim-to-real divide, that must be addressed for 3D generation to become a dependable foundation for embodied intelligence. Our project page is at https://3dgen4robot.github.io.
